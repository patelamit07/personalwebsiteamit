Program Objectives


To have an appreciation for and understanding of both the achievements of AI and the theory underlying those achievements. To have an appreciation for the engineering issues underlying the design of AI systems. To have a basic proficiency in a traditional AI language including an ability to write simple to intermediate programs and an ability to understand code written in that language. To have a basic understanding of some of the more advanced topics of AI such as learning, natural language processing, agents and robotics, expert systems, and planning.



ELIGIBILITY


1.Prospective students should have passed 10+2 or equivalent examination from any recognized the school board, with Maths, Physics & Chemistry as subjects studied at the intermediate level.
2. A Minimum of 50% marks in 10+2 as qualifying criteria to secure admission in this degree course.
3.Qualifying and fulfilling the cutoff of SAGE University Entrance Exam.
